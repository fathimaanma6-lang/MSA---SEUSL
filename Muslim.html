About Us

Empowering Students Through Collaborative Learning

The Muslim Student Association of South Eastern University of Sri Lanka developed this platform to help undergraduates improve their academic knowledge, research skills, presentation skills, and career readiness through educational activities and collaborative learning.

  • ✓
    Support undergraduate education across all faculties
  • ✓

  • ✓
    Share academic papers and learning materials freely
  • ✓
    Improve research and communication skills
  • ✓
    Build an educated, connected student community

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join the MSA-SEUSL platform? +

Any undergraduate student of South Eastern University of Sri Lanka can join our platform for free. We welcome students from all faculties and departments.

How do I register for seminars? +

Click the "Register Now" button on any event card. You'll receive a confirmation email with the Zoom link or venue details depending on the event type.

Are the resources free to download? +

Yes! All resources in our library are completely free for registered SEUSL students. Simply create an account and you can download anything instantly.

Will I receive a certificate for attending? +

Events marked with the 🎓 Certificate badge issue digital certificates upon attendance. Certificates are emailed within 3–5 working days after the event.

Can I upload my own study materials? +

Absolutely! Students can contribute notes, papers, and resources. Submissions are reviewed by our academic team before being published to ensure quality.

How can I verify my certificate? +

Use our Certificate Verification portal below. Enter your unique certificate code to verify its authenticity instantly.
